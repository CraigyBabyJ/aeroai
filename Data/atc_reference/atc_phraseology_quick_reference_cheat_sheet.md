# ATC Phraseology – Clean Quick Reference

*A practical, readable reference for pilots and virtual ATC use.*

---

## How to Read This
- **Italic text** = replace with your actual details
- **Readback required** items must be repeated exactly
- Don’t abuse **“roger”** or **“wilco”** where readback is required
- When in doubt: clarity > speed

---

## 1. General Phraseology

| Phrase | Meaning | Notes |
|------|-------|------|
| **Affirm / Affirmative** | Yes | |
| **Negative** | No | |
| **Roger** | I received your message | ❌ Not for readbacks |
| **Wilco** | Will comply | ❌ Not for readbacks |
| **Say again** | Repeat last transmission | |
| **Standby** | Please wait | Often ATC is busy |
| **Correction** | Disregard previous words | Replaces last instruction |
| **Unable, *reason*** | Cannot comply | Safety-related |

### Frequency Change
- **ATC:** *Callsign*, contact *station* on *frequency*
- **Pilot:** Contact *station* on *frequency*, *callsign*

## 2. Clearance Delivery

### Radio Check
- **Pilot:** *Station*, *callsign*, radio check
- **ATC:** *Callsign*, read you *1–5*

### IFR Clearance
- **Pilot:** *Station*, *callsign*, stand *number*, *aircraft type*, information *letter*, request IFR clearance to *destination*
- **ATC:** *Callsign*, cleared to *destination*, SID *name*, squawk *code*
- **Pilot (readback):** Cleared to *destination* via SID *name*, squawk *code*, *callsign*

### Pushback
- **Pilot:** *Callsign*, request pushback
- **ATC:** Pushback approved, facing *direction*
- **Pilot (readback):** Pushback approved, facing *direction*, *callsign*

### Engine Start
- **Pilot:** *Callsign*, request startup
- **ATC:** Startup approved
- **Pilot:** Startup approved, *callsign*

---

## 3. Taxi

### Standard Taxi
- **Pilot:** *Station*, *callsign*, stand *number*, request taxi
- **ATC:** Taxi to holding point runway *number* via *taxiways*
- **Pilot (readback):** Taxi to holding point runway *number* via *taxiways*, *callsign*

### Progressive Taxi
- **Pilot:** Request progressive taxi
- **ATC:** Expect progressive taxi
- **ATC:** Turn left/right now
- **Pilot:** Turning left/right, *callsign*

### Give Way
- **ATC:** Give way to *aircraft type* on *taxiway*
- **Pilot:** Will give way to *aircraft type*, *callsign*

### Runway Crossing
- **Pilot:** Holding short runway *number* at *intersection*
- **ATC:** Cross runway *number* at *intersection*
- **Pilot (readback):** Cross runway *number* at *intersection*, *callsign*

### Hold Instructions
- **ATC:** Hold short of *position*
- **Pilot:** Holding short of *position*, *callsign*

- **ATC:** Hold position
- **Pilot:** Holding position, *callsign*

---

## 4. Takeoff & Departure

### Line Up
- **ATC:** Line up and wait runway *number*
- **Pilot (readback):** Line up and wait runway *number*, *callsign*

⚠️ *Not a takeoff clearance*

### Takeoff Clearance
- **ATC:** Wind *direction* at *speed* knots, runway *number*, cleared for takeoff
- **Pilot (readback):** Cleared for takeoff runway *number*, *callsign*

### Cancel Takeoff (Before Roll)
- **ATC:** Cancel takeoff, I say again cancel takeoff, *reason*
- **Pilot:** Holding position, *callsign*

### Abort Takeoff (During Roll)
- **ATC:** Stop immediately!
- **Pilot:** Stopping, *callsign*

---

## 5. Climb, Cruise & Descent

### Initial Contact After Handoff
- **Pilot:** *Station*, *callsign*, passing *altitude*, climbing/descending to *cleared altitude*, *assigned SID/STAR*
- **ATC:** Radar contact, *new instructions*

### Altitude Change
- **ATC:** Climb/descend and maintain *altitude*
- **Pilot (readback):** Climb/descend and maintain *altitude*, *callsign*

### Heading Change
- **ATC:** Turn left/right heading *number*
- **Pilot (readback):** Turn left/right heading *number*, *callsign*

### Direct Routing
- **ATC:** Proceed direct *fix/navaid*
- **Pilot:** Proceeding direct *fix/navaid*, *callsign*

---

## 6. Approach & Landing

### ILS Clearance
- **ATC:** Cleared for ILS approach runway *number*
- **Pilot (readback):** Cleared for ILS approach runway *number*, *callsign*

### Localizer Report
- **ATC:** Report established localizer runway *number*
- **Pilot:** Will report established, *callsign*

### Continue Approach
- **ATC:** Continue approach
- **Pilot:** Continue approach, *callsign*

⚠️ *Not a landing clearance*

### Landing Clearance
- **ATC:** Wind *direction* at *speed* knots, runway *number*, cleared to land
- **Pilot (readback):** Cleared to land runway *number*, *callsign*

### Go-Around
- **ATC:** Go around
- **Pilot:** Going around, *callsign*

OR (pilot-initiated):
- **Pilot:** Going around
- **ATC:** Roger

## 7. Emergency Phraseology

### Mayday (Immediate Danger)
- **Pilot:** Mayday, Mayday, Mayday, *station*, *callsign*, *nature*, *intentions*
- **ATC:** Roger, squawk 7700, *instructions*

### Pan-Pan (Urgent, Not Immediate)
- **Pilot:** Pan-pan, Pan-pan, Pan-pan, *station*, *callsign*, *nature*, *intentions*

### TCAS RA
- **Pilot:** TCAS RA
- **Pilot:** Unable, TCAS RA
- **Pilot (after):** Clear of conflict, returning to assigned clearance

⚠️ TCAS instructions override ATC

---

## 8. Ground ↔ Tower Coordination (Reference)

### Runway Crossing Coordination
Ground and Tower coordinate internally before issuing crossing clearance to aircraft.

### Change of Active Runways
Tower informs Ground of new arrival and departure runways via intercom, with readback.
